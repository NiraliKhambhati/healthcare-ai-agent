HEDIS CBP - Controlling Blood Pressure
Eligible population: Adults 18-85 years with hypertension diagnosis on two separate visits.
Control threshold: Blood pressure reading below 140/90 mmHg.
Exclusions: ESRD, dialysis, kidney transplant, hospice, pregnancy-related hypertension.
Measurement: Most recent BP reading during the measurement year.

HEDIS CDC - Comprehensive Diabetes Care
Eligible population: Adults 18-75 years with diabetes diagnosis.
Measures include: HbA1c testing, eye exams, kidney disease monitoring, BP control.
HbA1c control threshold: Less than 8% for good control.
Exclusions: Patients in hospice care.

HEDIS AMR - Asthma Medication Ratio
Eligible population: Ages 5-64 with persistent asthma.
Measure: Ratio of controller medications to total asthma medications.
Target: Ratio of 0.50 or higher indicates good asthma control.
Exclusions: COPD diagnosis, hospice care.

HEDIS FUH - Follow-Up After Hospitalization for Mental Illness
Eligible population: Patients discharged from inpatient mental health stay.
Measure: Follow-up visit within 7 and 30 days of discharge.
Target conditions: Major depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia.
Exclusions: Patients who died during measurement year.

HEDIS BCS - Breast Cancer Screening
Eligible population: Women aged 50-74 years.
Measure: Mammography screening within the past 27 months.
Target: At least one mammogram recorded during measurement period.
Exclusions: Bilateral mastectomy, hospice care.

HEDIS COL - Colorectal Cancer Screening
Eligible population: Adults 45-75 years.
Measure: Appropriate colorectal cancer screening completed.
Accepted tests: Colonoscopy every 10 years, FIT test annually, CT colonography every 5 years.
Exclusions: Colorectal cancer diagnosis, total colectomy, hospice care.

HEDIS HbA1c Testing - Diabetes HbA1c Poor Control
Eligible population: Adults 18-75 years with diabetes.
Poor control threshold: HbA1c greater than 9% indicates poor glycemic control.
Measure: Percentage of diabetic patients with HbA1c above 9% — lower rate is better.
Exclusions: Hospice care enrollment.

HEDIS MMA - Medication Management for People with Asthma
Eligible population: Ages 5-64 with persistent asthma prescribed asthma controller medication.
Measure: Patients who remained on controller medication for at least 75% of treatment period.
Target: 75% or higher medication possession ratio.
Exclusions: COPD, emphysema, hospice care.

CIHI HSMR - Hospital Standardized Mortality Ratio
Definition: Ratio of actual deaths in hospital to expected deaths based on patient mix.
Target: HSMR below 100 indicates better than expected mortality performance.
Key diagnoses tracked: Sepsis, pneumonia, heart failure, stroke, hip fracture.
Used by: Canadian Institute for Health Information to benchmark Canadian hospitals.
Data source: Discharge Abstract Database (DAD).

CIHI Readmission Indicator
Definition: Unplanned readmission to any acute care hospital within 30 days of discharge.
Tracked conditions: COPD, heart failure, hip fracture, stroke, diabetes, mental health.
Target: Readmission rate below provincial average.
Canadian benchmark: National average 30-day readmission rate is approximately 8.5%.
Data source: CIHI Discharge Abstract Database (DAD).

CIHI Patient Safety Indicators
Definition: Measures of adverse events and patient safety incidents in Canadian hospitals.
Key indicators: Surgical site infections, medication errors, falls with injury, pressure injuries.
Reporting: Mandatory reporting to provincial health authorities.
Framework: Based on Canadian Patient Safety Institute (CPSI) guidelines.

CIHI ALC - Alternate Level of Care
Definition: Patients occupying acute care beds while waiting for more appropriate care setting.
Target: ALC rate below 15% of total patient days.
Impact: High ALC rates indicate capacity pressures and care transition gaps.
Common destinations: Long-term care, home care, rehabilitation facilities.
Canadian context: ALC is a major challenge in Canadian hospitals due to aging population.

ICD-10 Heart Failure Codes
Primary code: I50 - Heart failure.
Subtypes: I50.0 Congestive heart failure, I50.1 Left ventricular failure, I50.9 Heart failure unspecified.
Related codes: I11.0 Hypertensive heart disease with heart failure, I13.0 Hypertensive heart and renal disease.
Used in: HEDIS, CIHI readmission tracking, CMS quality programs.
30-day readmission risk: Approximately 25% for heart failure patients.

ICD-10 Diabetes Codes
Primary code: E11 - Type 2 diabetes mellitus.
Subtypes: E11.0 with hyperosmolarity, E11.1 with ketoacidosis, E11.65 with hyperglycemia.
Complications: E11.2 kidney complications, E11.3 ophthalmic complications, E11.4 neurological complications.
Used in: HEDIS CDC measure, chronic disease management programs.
HbA1c monitoring: Required every 3-6 months for uncontrolled diabetes.

ICD-10 Mental Health Codes
Major depression: F32 depressive episode, F33 recurrent depressive disorder.
Bipolar disorder: F31 bipolar affective disorder.
Schizophrenia: F20 schizophrenia, F25 schizoaffective disorder.
Anxiety: F41 other anxiety disorders, F40 phobic anxiety disorders.
Used in: HEDIS FUH measure, mental health quality reporting.
Follow-up requirement: 7-day and 30-day post-discharge visits mandatory for quality compliance.

ICD-10 Hypertension Codes
Primary code: I10 - Essential (primary) hypertension.
Secondary: I15 - Secondary hypertension.
With heart disease: I11 - Hypertensive heart disease.
With renal disease: I12 - Hypertensive renal disease.
Used in: HEDIS CBP measure, cardiovascular risk stratification.
Blood pressure target: Below 140/90 mmHg for HEDIS compliance.

ICD-10 Readmission Tracking Codes
Sepsis: A41.9 sepsis unspecified, A41.0 sepsis due to Staphylococcus aureus.
Pneumonia: J18.9 pneumonia unspecified, J18.1 lobar pneumonia.
COPD exacerbation: J44.1 COPD with acute exacerbation.
Stroke: I63 cerebral infarction, I64 stroke not specified as hemorrhage or infarction.
Hip fracture: S72 fracture of femur, commonly used in elderly fall tracking.
